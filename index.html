Meet DJ C-Four

More than a DJ.
An experience.

With over a decade in the entertainment industry, DJ C-Four has become one of the Caribbean's most versatile and sought-after DJs.

His journey began long before the booth through classical piano training and live musical performance. That foundation now fuels his ability to blend genres, read crowds and create memorable moments with precision.

From intimate celebrations to major Carnival stages, every performance is curated to deliver what has become known as The Explosive Experience.
